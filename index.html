Hi, I am

Ganesh Pawar

I am a PhD student at Centrum Astronomiczne im. M. Kopernika PAN (CAMK, Toruń) working with Prof. Krzysztof Hełminiak. My current research focus is on measuring the fundamental properties of eclipsing binary stars. I obtained my B.Sc. degree from the K J Somaiya College of Science & Commerce (KJSSC) and M.Sc. degree from the Department of Physics, University of Mumbai (UDP). I was also a visiting project student at the Aryabhatta Research Institute of observational Sciences (ARIES).

This website is my personal space where one may find articles/information related to my research and outreach, sometimes aimed for general understanding. Besides astronomy, I like to spend my time in watching movies, reading books, outreach activities, trekking and solving and exploring problems. I'd like to hear from people if they have any advice or a query or a job. You can use the above social media buttons.
