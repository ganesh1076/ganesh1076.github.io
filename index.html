Research

Research

My research is currently focused on the stellar activity of ‘B, A and F’ type stars using TESS data to determine the pulsation frequency. I am also reducing spectroscopic data of ‘Be’ stars taken from IUCAA Girawali Observatory by writing python codes to extract a Continuum normalized spectrum to check the line variation, we are also using synthetic spectra to find the physical parameters like Teff, projected rotational velocity, logg, etc. I have just started reducing the physical parameters from high-resolution spectroscopy using HERMES data.
